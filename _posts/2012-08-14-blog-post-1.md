Art of reading charts as looking as a fine painting
Many times future posts here will probably refer to FIFO scalping as AN ART FORM and some may not understand that concept coming into this thread with a technical or fundamental background. I know in the beginning it was difficult for me having been accustomed to trading with lots of lagging indicators hoping for Buy and Sell signals and not understanding how the market actually works.

When you trash most of that junk and start to focus and learn PA and volume and how they act together and respond to key levels as support and resistance you will acquire a instinct by glancing at a chart (as looking at a fine painting) and be able to see trades quickly with lots of practice.

It is not easy and takes time, but to become a Lawyer or Doctor or any professional, one must dedicate lots of time and energy to become successful and it's the same with any opportunity that offers a nice future and a nice comfortable income.
